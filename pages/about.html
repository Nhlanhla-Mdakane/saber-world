About Us

Who are We?

We are a small company based in Cape Town South Africa and we supply most possibly the greatest and coolest weapon in the Galaxy, The LightSaber. As Avid Star Wars fans ,we thought it was not right for the power of the Lightsabers to be only held by the Jedi and Sith, so we decided (through hard work and creativity) to create our own replica Lightsaber's so that the entire galaxy can enjoy these works of Art,
